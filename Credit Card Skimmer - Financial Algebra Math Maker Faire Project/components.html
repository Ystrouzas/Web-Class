Credit Card Skimmer Detector

Screen

The screen used in this device is a 0.96" OLED display from Adafruit. It was used due to its small form factor, accetable viewing angles, and compatability and ease of use with the Raspberry Pi Zero W.

View details »

Ports

The bottom of the device provides access to the ports avalable on the Raspberry Pi Zero W computer, and the power distribution board. The Raspberry Pi provides includes a mini HDMI for video out, and two micro-USB ports, one ordinary port and one to power the device off of the wall.

View details »

Battery

The final micro-USB port accessable on the bottom the device belongs to the power distribution board. This port only allows the user to charge the 1200mAh battery. Next to this port is the power button used to toggle power to the device. A single click turns the device on and a double click turns the device off.

Ample Accessories: You will be prepared

Upon purchase you will receive a one Credit Card Skimmer Detector, a Micro-USB charging cable, 5 Volt USB wall charging adapter, a 12V to 5V USB Car charger, and a climate control vent mount.

Rechargable Battery: Use the detector on the go

This device was designed with portability in mind, and to this effect we have manufactured our product to fit inside of a sleek, small, durable, and customizable case, and include a "large" 1200 mAh rechargeable Lithium Ion battery to ensure you will always have enough power to last the day.

Ample Ports Allowing connection

Located on the bottom of the device is the power button, three micro-USB ports (1 charging port, power port, and 1 USB port to interface with the device), and one mini-HDMI port.
