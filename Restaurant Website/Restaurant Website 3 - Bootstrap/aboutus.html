About Us

Sparky's Giant Burgers is located in Oakland, California. We sell hamburgers, hot dogs, french fries, sandwiches, salads, dinners, deserts, and beverages. We were established in 2000 by Min Ho. We offer indoor and outdoor seating, and a private parking lot located in front of our building. You can order take out by calling (510) 531-5617

We are located at 4120 Redwood Road, Oakland at Lincoln Square Center
